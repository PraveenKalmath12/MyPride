Mypride Ludo
LudoGame
"MyPride Ludo" brings the timeless excitement of the classic board game to a whole new level. In this exhilarating race to the finish, players compete with up to four opponents to navigate their tokens from start to finish, all while showcasing their strategic prowess and asserting their pride. As the game begins, each player selects their color and places their four tokens in their respective starting zones.
Mypride Rummy
RummyGame
"MyPride Rummy" is a strategic card game for 2 to 6 players. Players aim to form sets and runs of cards from a standard deck. The game combines skillful play with calculated risks as players strive to be the first to lay down all their cards. Victory hinges on smart decisions and tactical discards.
Mypride Poker
PokerGame
"MyPride Poker"is an exciting card game where players strive to showcase their strategic prowess and confidence at the poker table. Competing against 2 to 8 players, participants aim to build the strongest hand possible using a variety of poker variants such as Texas Hold'em, Omaha, or Seven-Card Stud. With each hand, players bet, bluff, and make calculated decisions to outsmart their opponents and emerge victorious.
